Luis Villafana Tamez

Hello! My name is Luis Villafana Tamez and I study Computer Programming at Georgian College in Barrie, Ontario. Previously, I earned a Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering and a Bachelor of Science in Bioengineering, both from the University of Illinois at Chicago. My main interests are nature, computational science, and computational art. In this web page, I have put together a collection of some of my co-curricular and extra-curricular projects.

Algorithms and mechanisms

  1. An algorithm to compute the Voronoi graph of an arbitrary set of points and line segments by Steiner point insertion
  2. An algorithm to transform a digital wireframe to a 3D printable solid truss
  3. A mechanism to produce a gas with a specified oxygen concentration by mixing
